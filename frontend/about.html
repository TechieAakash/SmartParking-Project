Our Strategic Pillars

E-Governance
Transparent, paperless revenue collection for MCD through digital audits, ensuring fiscal responsibility and zero leakage.
Smart Mobility
Real-time parking availability to reduce "cruising time" for vehicles, directly lowering fuel consumption and traffic congestion.
Sustainable Environment
Integration with EV charging stations to support Delhi's green transition and reduce urban air pollution.
Safety & Security
AI-based ANPR (Automatic Number Plate Recognition) and 24/7 surveillance for a safer city environment.
