Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for parking?
Payments are handled automatically via your vehicle's FASTag. Ensure your FASTag has sufficient balance. For non-FASTag vehicles, you can use the MCD311 app QR scan at the zone entrance.
What is a 'Cruising Fine'?
Cruising occurs when a vehicle remains in a high-demand zone without a valid parking session for more than 15 minutes. This is detected by AI vision and leads to an automatic violation report.
How do I dispute a violation?
You can dispute a violation via the Complaints Centre (redirect from footer) within 48 hours. Provide your violation code and a brief explanation. Our bureau reviews AI evidence before processing.

Still need assistance?

Our support bureau is available 24/7 for technical and payment issues.
