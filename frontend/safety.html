Safety Guidelines for Users

Important Note: Under the new MCD safety policy, all parking sites are under 30-day CCTV surveillance. Footage can be requested via the helpline for any reported incidents.

Panic Buttons: Located at every ticket kiosk and main pillar. Press in case of an emergency.
Verified Staff: Only engage with parking attendants wearing valid MCD Identity Cards.
Report Overstay: If you see a vehicle abandoned for more than 48 hours, please report it via 155305 to help us keep slots available for genuine users.
