Supply to MCD: Vendor Checklist

Ensure your business meets the rigorous standards of municipal infrastructure supply.

01

Legal & Financials

GST registration, 3-year audited financial statements, and GeM listing.

02

Technical Compliance

ISO 9001/14001 certifications. Hardware must meet CPWD electrical and safety specifications.

03

"Make in India" Badge

Priority is given to vendors with local manufacturing facilities as per the DPIIT Public Procurement Order.

04

Service Backbone

Must have a dedicated local support team in Delhi NCT for rapid on-site resolution.
