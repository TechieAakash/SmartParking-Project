Privacy Notice

The Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

What Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide smart parking services, including:

  • Vehicle Registration Number (License Plate)
  • Contact details (Name, Phone Number, Email)
  • Payment transaction history (Note: We do not store full credit card details)
  • Location data when using the "Find Parking" or "Navigation" features

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Processing parking reservations and payments.
  • Enforcing parking regulations and issuing penalty notices.
  • Improving traffic management across New Delhi.
  • Sending service notifications and alerts.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared with:

  • Law enforcement agencies (e.g., Delhi Traffic Police) when required by law.
  • Authorized payment gateways for transaction processing.
  • Third-party contractors strictly for operational maintenance under NDA.

Data Security

All data is transmitted via secure SSL encryption. We employ industry-standard firewalls and access controls to prevent unauthorized access to our systems.
